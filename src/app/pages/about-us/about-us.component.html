About Us

Origins twist was born out of a desire to create beautifully made reusable coffee cups with our signature theme of only designing with African Wax print.

Our eco-friendly cups come in exuberant ethnic print, inspired by the African wax print designs, there by bringing together two themes that we are really passionate about.

Our goal is to continue the conversation of this behavioural change from discarding to reusing. Whilst introducing a whole new element of doing it in style. Our beautiful designs are sure to be a show stopper.
